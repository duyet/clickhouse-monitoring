## Release cadence

- **Stable images** are published to `duyet/clickhouse-monitoring:latest` on every push to `main`.
- **Tagged releases** (`vX.Y.Z`) are cut periodically and mirrored to the Helm chart and Docker tags.
- Follow [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/duyet/clickhouse-monitoring/releases) for the full per-tag changelog.
