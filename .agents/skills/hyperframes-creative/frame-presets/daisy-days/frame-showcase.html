Rules

Frame Rules

Do
  • Fredoka headlines, Quicksand body — strict by role.
  • Outline every shape 3px charcoal + hard offset shadow (6/4px, no blur).
  • Headlines on saturated surfaces get a 3px charcoal text-shadow + white text.
  • Bullets are outlined butter discs; cluster 3–7 ornaments per frame.
  • One content container per frame; rotate marker colors through the pastels.
Don't
  • No square corners; no blurred or rgba shadows.
  • No colored borders (charcoal only); no coral surface.
  • No third font; no Quicksand headline or Fredoka body.
  • No glyph bullets; no empty corners (ornaments fill them).
  • Don't blow a headline edge-to-edge — fit to measure.
