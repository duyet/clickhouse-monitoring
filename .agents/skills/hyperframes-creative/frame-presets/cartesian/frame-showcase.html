Frame Rules

Restraint

Do

  • Use a single 1px taupe line for every separator — the hairline is the identity.
  • Set every Playfair headline at weight 400, in ink, sentence case.
  • Render labels in taupe, uppercase, 2–3px tracked.
  • Layer one or two compass rings (solid + dashed) at 20–50% behind content.
  • Let frames breathe — sparse, generous negative space.

Don't

  • No populist accent color — no red, blue, green; stone and ink only.
  • No bold Playfair, no taupe headlines, no thick borders.
  • No shadows, elevated cards, or rounded rectangles (circles only).
  • Don't crowd the frame — packed layouts read as broken.
  • Never more than two geo decorations per frame.
