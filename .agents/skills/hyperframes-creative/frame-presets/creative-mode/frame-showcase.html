Frame Rules

Do

  • One idea per frame — two things means two frames.
  • Lean centered; reserve the green ground for the closing plate.
  • Two or three accents per frame, never all four.
  • 4px ink borders, 24px hard offset on featured blocks only.
  • Archivo Black uppercase, 0.92 line-height, always.

Don't

  • No rounded corners except the topbar pill.
  • No gradients, no blurred shadows, no glow.
  • No Archivo Black in sentence case, ever.
  • No fifth accent; no pure-white background.
  • No headline blown edge-to-edge — size to the line.
