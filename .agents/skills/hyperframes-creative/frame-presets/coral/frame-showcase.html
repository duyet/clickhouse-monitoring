Frame Rules

Flat · hard-edge · coral

Do

  • Compose as multi-surface region splits — coral, ink, cream at hard edges.
  • Set every Bebas element uppercase with 1–12px letter-spacing.
  • Render eyebrows in coral on cream/ink, in ink on coral.
  • Apply the 45° hatch at 6% opacity on coral regions.
  • Fill underweight coral regions with a 12%-opacity wallpaper numeral.

Don't

  • Never render Bebas in sentence case or without tracking.
  • No fourth surface; no drop shadows, elevations, or rounded rectangles.
  • No white headlines on coral — Bebas on coral is always ink.
  • No gray headlines; gray is body and meta only.
  • Don't pair Bebas with a different body sans than Inter.
